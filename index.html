Jammu & Kashmir's ODOP program is a vibrant showcase of the region's unique crafts and products. It's a burst of local pride, empowering artisans and entrepreneurs while preserving traditional skills. This initiative not only boosts the local economy but also celebrates the rich cultural heritage of the area, district by district.
